"CREATIVITY SIMMERS FOR YOU"

Welcome to the Stockpot Portfolio!

I’m Noel Eldred, the mind behind Stockpot Designs and I know what you’re thinking: “That’s a weird name.”

And to that I say: “Yes. Yes it is.”

The name Stockpot, while odd, perfectly represents me and my history as an artist. Growing up, my parents would often make hearty meals by throwing whatever was in the fridge and cabinets into a big pot. Somehow, this random amalgamation of ingredients would turn into a beautiful and delicious soup. Much like my parent’s approach to making soup, my approach to making art is to cram as many styles, methods, and mediums into my head as possible (with a dash of inspiration) in the hopes that I’ll create a beautiful and delicious piece of art. Well, maybe not delicious. Unless you plan to print out and eat my art, which respectfully, I don’t classify as my business.

Anyways, as a graphic designer and artist, it is my goal to create as much as I can in this lifetime and explore as many art forms and styles as possible. Art has always been my passion and since you’ve made it here, I hope to share that with you!

This is my personal portfolio website. Here you’ll find a collection of my designs and art, as well as get to know more about me as a creative and my qualifications. If you have any questions or are looking to hire me, please do so through the contact form!

Have fun exploring!
